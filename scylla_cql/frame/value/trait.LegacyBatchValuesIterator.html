An iterator-like for ValueList

An instance of this can be easily obtained from IT: Iterator<Item: ValueList>: that would be BatchValuesIteratorFromIterator<IT>

Itâ€™s just essentially making methods from ValueList accessible instead of being an actual iterator because of compiler limitations that would otherwise be very complex to overcome. (specifically, types being different would require yielding enums for tuple impls)
